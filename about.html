Our Mission

Emergency information
that works when it matters most

Helper-ID exists because the moment a first responder needs your information is exactly the moment you can't provide it yourself. We built a simple, physical solution to a problem that affects everyone.

The Problem

Your phone can't help when it's dead, locked, or missing.

[PLACEHOLDER] Describe the origin of Helper-ID — the moment, the scenario, or the person that made the problem real. What happened that made this feel urgent?

Emergency contacts stored in phones are inaccessible in the moments they're needed most. Medical information written on paper gets lost. ICE contacts require a working, unlocked phone. Helper-ID is the physical backup that doesn't depend on any of that.

The Solution

A tag that speaks for you when you can't.

Helper-ID is a physical NFC tag or card — worn, carried, or kept close — that gives first responders, family members, and bystanders instant access to the information they need. No app required. No phone required. No guessing.

What We Believe

Principles we build by

🧭

Physical First

Digital systems fail. Batteries die. Signals drop. A physical tag works in every scenario — it doesn't need power, signal, or a working phone to do its job.

🔓

Your Data, Your Control

You decide what's on your profile. You decide who can see it. We don't sell your information, share it with third parties, or use it for anything other than helping you be found and helped.

🤝

Built for Communities

Helper-ID is most powerful when a whole community has it — families, neighborhoods, assisted living facilities, athletic programs. We design for adoption, not just individuals.

⚡

Radically Simple

A first responder in a stressful situation shouldn't have to figure out your technology. Tap, read, act. That's the entire user experience for the person who needs it most.

💰

Accessible Pricing

Emergency preparedness shouldn't be a luxury. We keep pricing honest — a free PDF option, a one-time purchase path, and a membership for those who want the full hosted experience.

🛡️

Quietly Reliable

You set it up once and don't think about it again. It's there when you need it. That's the whole promise — not an app you have to remember to update, but a system that just works.

Built By

An Empathy Lab project

Helper-ID is a product of Empathy Lab, an independent lab focused on building tools that solve real human problems — not hypothetical ones. We build things for the moments that matter: emergencies, transitions, and the times when people need help they didn't ask for.
