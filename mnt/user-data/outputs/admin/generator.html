📱 Writing the Tag — Step by Step

1
Tap Copy URL above.
2
Open NFC Tools on your Android phone.
3
Tap Write → Add a record → URL / URI → paste the URL.
4
Tap Write / OK, then hold the NTAG215 tag to the back of the phone.
5
Hand the tag to the member. Have them tap it with their own phone to confirm it loads. ✅
6
Collect payment if not already done. Mark as complete.
