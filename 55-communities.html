What is Helper-ID?

Simple by design. Powerful when it matters.

Helper-ID is a simple way to make sure the right people can help you if you ever need it — without changing how you live your life.

1

You create your profile

Add the information you choose to share — emergency contacts, medical notes, allergy information, helpful instructions.

2

You get a CODE + PIN

Each Helper-ID has a unique CODE and PIN. Keep it where you want — your wallet, a keychain, a bracelet, a sticker, or printed on your fridge.

3

Help arrives instantly

Someone enters your CODE + PIN at helper-id.com. Your profile opens. Only what you chose to share is shown. That's it.
